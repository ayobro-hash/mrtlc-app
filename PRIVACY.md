# Privacy Policy for MRTLC // CORE

**Last updated: June 13, 2026**

MRTLC // CORE ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data.

### 1. Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. MRTLC // CORE is an offline-first application. All processing of files—including `.rbxm` files—is performed locally on your device.

### 2. Local File Access
To function, the app requires permission to access your device’s storage. This is strictly for the purpose of allowing you to select and process the specific files you choose. We do not access files outside of your explicit selections, and we never upload your files to any remote server or third-party cloud.

### 3. Internet Usage
The application does not require an internet connection to perform its core functions. We do not track your usage, nor do we use analytics software to collect data about your activity.

### 4. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party services that collect, store, or transmit your data.

### 5. Contact Information
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository:
https://github.com/ayobro-hash/mrtlc-app
